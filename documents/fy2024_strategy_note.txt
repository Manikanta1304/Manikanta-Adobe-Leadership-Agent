ACME CORPORATION — FY2024 STRATEGIC PRIORITIES
===============================================
Prepared by: Office of the CEO
Date: January 2024

VISION
------
Become the #1 mid-market cloud infrastructure provider in North America by 2027,
with $1B in ARR and operations across 12 countries.

STRATEGIC PILLARS FOR 2024
---------------------------

1. PRODUCT LEADERSHIP
   Priority: Ship CloudEdge v3.0 by Q3 2024 (now delayed to Q4 — see Q3 report).
   Investment: $22M R&D budget, 40% increase over FY2023.
   Target: Achieve feature parity with NovaTech by H1 2024, surpass by H2 2024.

2. ENTERPRISE EXPANSION
   Target: Grow Enterprise segment from $165M to $210M ARR (+27%).
   Strategy: Hire 25 senior AEs focused on Fortune 1000 accounts.
   Key markets: Financial services, healthcare, public sector.

3. INTERNATIONAL GROWTH
   EMEA launch: Q2 2024 (completed — underperforming, see Q3 report).
   APAC launch: Q3 2024 (completed — early stage, below initial targets).
   Dedicated regional GMs hired for both regions.

4. OPERATIONAL EXCELLENCE
   Cost reduction target: $12M annualised savings by year-end.
   Initiatives: Procurement consolidation, cloud spend optimization,
   real-estate rationalisation (2 offices closing H2 2024).

5. TALENT & CULTURE
   Goal: Reduce attrition to below 10% by year-end (currently 14.2% — off track).
   Programs: Updated compensation bands (effective Q4 2024),
   flexible-work policy revision, employee equity refresh.

REVENUE TARGETS BY SEGMENT (FY2024)
-------------------------------------
Cloud Services:      $265M  (FY2023: $228M, +16%)
Enterprise Sales:    $175M  (FY2023: $170M, +3%)  ← at-risk given Q3 miss
Consumer Products:   $ 88M  (FY2023: $ 84M, +5%)
Professional Svcs:   $ 52M  (FY2023: $ 58M, -10%)  ← restructuring planned
TOTAL:               $580M

M&A PIPELINE
------------
The Board approved exploration of two acquisition targets in Q1 2024:
- DataBridge Inc. (data integration middleware) — LOI signed, due diligence ongoing
- CloudMonitor GmbH (EU observability tooling) — initial discussions underway

Either acquisition, if completed, would accelerate EU expansion and
AI Act compliance readiness.

BOARD-LEVEL CONCERNS RAISED IN JANUARY 2024 OFFSITE
-----------------------------------------------------
1. Competitive pricing pressure — leadership must decide on a pricing response to
   NovaTech's aggressive market entry by Q4 2024.
2. Over-reliance on US market — 83% of revenue is US-sourced; diversification urgent.
3. AI integration into product — board expects a credible AI roadmap by end of Q3 2024.
   (Roadmap was presented in September 2024; details in the AI Strategy Note.)
